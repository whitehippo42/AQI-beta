Project Goals

Build and test a machine learning model that can predict air quality

  • Specific: Train and evaluate at least two machine learning models (e.g., Random Forest, XGBoost) for AQI prediction using publicly available datasets.
  • Measurable: Achieve an R² score of at least 0.75 on the test dataset.
  • Achievable: We have access to the required data and skills to execute this task.
  • Relevant: Model accuracy is essential for providing reliable forecasts.
  • Time-bound: Complete model training and evaluation by end of July.
